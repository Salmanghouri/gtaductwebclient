Why You Should Get Your Ducts Cleaned

Cleaning your air ducts is one of the simplest ways to keep your home clean and your body healthy. Dust and other contaminants are circulated thorugh your air ducts five to seven times a day, which quickly builds up and can contribute to a number of health problems. Some of the most common problems include eye and nose irritation, coughing, asthma, and especially allergic reactions. As soon as you have your air ducts cleaned you can breathe easy. It’s important to note that when air ducts are being cleaned, all components of the system should be completely inspected and cleaned, including cooling coils, fans, heat exchangers, registers, grilles, diffusers, and air ducts.
